# Brand Voice Guidelines

## Core Personality Traits
1. **Professional** - Knowledgeable and credible
2. **Approachable** - Friendly and welcoming
3. **Innovative** - Forward-thinking and creative
4. **Helpful** - Supportive and solution-oriented

## Tone Variations

### Formal Tone (LinkedIn, Whitepapers)
- Use complete sentences
- Professional vocabulary
- Data-driven insights
- Industry terminology

### Casual Tone (Twitter, Instagram)
- Conversational language
- Emojis appropriate to context
- Shorter sentences
- Engaging questions

### Educational Tone (Blogs, How-tos)
- Clear explanations
- Step-by-step guidance
- Practical examples
- Actionable advice

## Language Guidelines

### Do:
- Use "we" and "our" for company perspective
- Include data and statistics when available
- Ask engaging questions to encourage interaction
- Use positive, solution-focused language
- Include calls-to-action

### Don't:
- Use jargon without explanation
- Make exaggerated claims
- Use negative or critical language about competitors
- Post without proofreading
- Ignore comments and engagement

## Visual Style

### Colors:
- Primary: #2A5CAA (Blue)
- Secondary: #FF6B35 (Orange)
- Neutral: #F5F7FA (Light Gray)

### Typography:
- Headlines: Montserrat Bold
- Body: Open Sans Regular
- Code: Roboto Mono

### Imagery:
- Professional photography
- Clean illustrations
- Data visualizations
- Branded templates

## Content Standards

### Quality Checklist:
- [ ] Accurate information
- [ ] Proper grammar and spelling
- [ ] Relevant hashtags
- [ ] Appropriate visual
- [ ] Clear call-to-action
- [ ] Mobile-friendly format
- [ ] Accessibility considered

### Approval Process:
1. Content creation
2. Brand compliance check
3. Legal review (if needed)
4. Scheduling
5. Post-publication monitoring

## Crisis Communication

### Response Protocol:
1. Acknowledge quickly
2. Investigate thoroughly
3. Respond appropriately
4. Follow up consistently
5. Learn and improve

### Escalation Matrix:
- Level 1: Social media manager
- Level 2: Marketing director
- Level 3: Executive team
- Level 4: Legal department
