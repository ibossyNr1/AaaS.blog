# Lead Scoring Template

Use this template to score and prioritize leads based on various factors.

## Scoring Criteria

### Demographic Factors (Max 40 points)
- **Job Title** (20 points)
  - C-Level: 20
  - VP/Director: 15
  - Manager: 10
  - Individual Contributor: 5
  - Other: 0

- **Company Size** (10 points)
  - Enterprise (1000+): 10
  - Mid-Market (100-999): 8
  - Small Business (10-99): 6
  - Startup (1-9): 4

- **Industry** (10 points)
  - Target Industry: 10
  - Related Industry: 7
  - Other Industry: 3

### Engagement Factors (Max 30 points)
- **Email Engagement** (15 points)
  - Opened multiple emails: 15
  - Opened one email: 10
  - Not opened: 0

- **Website Activity** (15 points)
  - Multiple page views: 15
  - Single page view: 8
  - No activity: 0

### Fit Factors (Max 30 points)
- **Technology Stack** (10 points)
  - Uses complementary tech: 10
  - Uses competitive tech: 0
  - Unknown: 5

- **Budget Alignment** (10 points)
  - Within budget range: 10
  - Above/below range: 5
  - Unknown: 3

- **Timeline** (10 points)
  - Immediate need: 10
  - 3-6 months: 7
  - 6+ months: 3
  - No timeline: 0

## Scoring Matrix

### Tier 1: Hot Leads (80-100 points)
- Immediate follow-up required
- High conversion probability
- Assign to top sales rep

### Tier 2: Warm Leads (60-79 points)
- Nurture campaign
- Regular follow-up
- Educational content

### Tier 3: Cold Leads (40-59 points)
- Long-term nurture
- Automated sequences
- Re-evaluate periodically

### Tier 4: Unqualified (0-39 points)
- Archive or disqualify
- May requalify later

## Next Steps Template

For each tier, use these follow-up actions:

**Tier 1 (Hot):**
- [ ] Call within 24 hours
- [ ] Send personalized proposal
- [ ] Schedule demo
- [ ] Connect on LinkedIn

**Tier 2 (Warm):**
- [ ] Send case studies
- [ ] Invite to webinar
- [ ] Share relevant content
- [ ] Follow up in 3-5 days

**Tier 3 (Cold):**
- [ ] Add to newsletter
- [ ] Send educational content
- [ ] Re-engage quarterly
- [ ] Monitor for triggers
