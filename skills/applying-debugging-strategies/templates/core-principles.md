# Core Debugging Principles

### 1. The Scientific Method
1. **Observe**: What's the actual behavior?
2. **Hypothesize**: What could be causing it?
3. **Experiment**: Test your hypothesis
4. **Analyze**: Did it prove/disprove your theory?
5. **Repeat**: Until you find the root cause

### 2. Debugging Mindset
- **Don't Assume**: Question everything.
- **Reproduce consistently**: Isolate the problem.
- **Keep detailed notes**: Document every step.
- **Question everything**: Assumption is the mother of all bugs.
- **Take breaks**: Fresh eyes solve problems faster.

### 3. Rubber Duck Debugging
Explain your code and problem out loud. Often reveals the hidden logic flaw.
